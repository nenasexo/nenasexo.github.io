About Peperuto

Peperuto is a virtual assistant Bot that will help you navigate the world of cryptocurrencies, you can play with him and take profits as it has a token with which you can invest in this project in various activities.

How it works

Peperuto offers you a variety of functions to keep you informed about cryptocurrencies. With the /trends command, you can find out the top trends in tokens, while with /mxtx, you can get the mxtx of tokens for your snipes. If you need to know the price of gas to make transactions, just use the /gas command and it will be a companion in your day to day life, it can answer some questions in the future. And that's not all, because throughout the project new functions will be added so that you can be aware of everything related to cryptocurrencies.

About the token

The $PEPERUTO token has great importance in the bot, it is used to pay for promotions, premiun commands, events and even in an upcoming NFT game, staking and farm on our website.
